title: SwingFish - Rip Grooveshark tags: grooveshark, ripper SwingFish is a tiny app with one function: to download songs from [Grooveshark](http://grooveshark.com). Check out [the screenshot and download it](/swingfish.html). ## Making it ## Writing SwingFish was simple: 1. Use Wireshark to sniff the traffic. 2. Create a stand-alone app to display Grooveshark. 3. Add a little code to rip the songs. That's really all there was to it. (This same technique works with Pandora too...)
