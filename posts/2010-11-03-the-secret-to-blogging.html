title: The Secret to Blogging tags: blogging, habits Today, as I was riding my bike to work, I realized that I must turn blogging into a habit if I want to create a successful blog. I've been reading a great e-book about productivity: "[Zen to Done](http://zenhabits.net/zen-to-done-the-simple-productivity-e-book/)". It's easy to read and what's written makes a lot of sense. The most important thing I've learned is that to be productive/successful you must form habits. I thought blogging would become easier if I made it a habit and decided I could probably find posts by bloggers to back this up. Lo and behold, I found three. * [8 Rules to Develop the Habit of Blogging](http://northxeast.com/8-rules-to-develop-the-habit-of-blogging/) * [10 Easy Ways to Get in The Habit of Blogging](http://www.lostartofblogging.com/10-easy-ways-to-get-in-the-habit-of-blogging) * [22 Essential Habits Towards Blogging Success](http://www.lostartofblogging.com/22-essential-habits-towards-blogging-success) There are others with great habits that bloggers have: * [The 8 Habits of Highly Effective Bloggers](http://www.copyblogger.com/effective-blog-habits/) * [10 Habits of Highly Effective ProBloggers](http://www.problogger.net/archives/2006/05/24/10-habits-of-highly-effective-probloggers/) The secret to blogging, at least when starting out, is making it a habit, a habit of posting.
