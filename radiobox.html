RadioBox

RadioBox is a remake of an app I made years ago for family and friends, but this time I'm releasing it to the world for all to enjoy. RadioBox displays Pandora's mini player in a window and downloads the songs that it plays.

Features

  • Songs are in the tiny M4A format (fully compatible with iPods).
  • Songs are tagged with title, artist, and album.
  • Songs include high-quality album art from LastFM, when possible.
  • When you mark a song "Thumbs down", the downloaded copy is deleted.

Download

As with MPlayer, check your local laws before using this app.

Download it now. (Requires OS X 10.5 or greater.)
